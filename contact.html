Frequently Asked Questions

☕ How can I place a delivery order?

You can order directly through our website menu page or call us at +20 1032581322 for delivery. Free delivery on orders over 200 EGP.

🚚 What is your delivery range?

We deliver within Assiut city limits. Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets (Vodafone Cash, InstaPay).

🎉 Can I book your cafe for private events?

Yes! Our cafe is available for private events, birthdays, and corporate gatherings. Contact us for a quote.

🔄 How do I report an issue with my order?

Contact our support team immediately via phone or email, and we'll resolve your issue within 30 minutes.
